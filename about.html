About Vivo

Vivo Education (VE), established in 2012, is an education consultancy which delivers two services: Test Preparation (IELTS/TOEFL/SAT) and Study Abroad Counselling (Australia,UK,USA,Canada,New Zealand). Located in the heart of kathmandu valley, Putalisadak. VE is commited to cater genuine, updated and quality service to prospective students who plan to shape thier academic career abroad. to our students

Vivo Education has established itself as a leading institution that provides honest, credible, and authentic eduation and migration counseling advice for studying around the globe. We offer end-to-end counseling services.

Our Mission

To provide students with a platform and the best possible options for their academic and career growth in collaboration with educational institutions and foster mutual growth through strong relationships with educational institutions built on honesty, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a trustworthy partner throughout students’ academic lives, empowering them with better education and practical experience opportunities to help them achieve their career goals.

Our Goal
