About Us

9+ years of Your Trust and Recommendation

Vivo Education (VE), established in 2012, is an education consultancy which delivers two services: Test Preparation (IELTS/TOEFL/SAT) and Study Abroad Counselling (Australia,UK,USA,Canada,New Zealand). Located in the heart of kathmandu valley, Putalisadak. VE is commited to cater genuine, updated and quality service to prospective students who plan to shape thier academic career abroad. to our students

Vivo Education has established itself as a leading institution that provides honest, credible, and authentic eduation and migration counseling advice for studying around the globe. We offer end-to-end counseling services.

Visa and Immigration

Your Journey towards your dream

Step: One

Book a counseling session

In addition to telephone & video counseling, Gateway International provides flexible time slots that are chosen by the user for guiding you through the various options for studying across the globe.

Step: Two

Admission Guidance

Whether you’re looking for a program, university, or country according to your profile, our career counselors can offer advice and guidance.

Step: Three

Submit your Application

We will assist you in submitting your applications at your preferred university for admission after you have selected the right course and university.

Step: Four

Letter of Acceptance

As soon as you submit your application, the university will issue an offer letter tailored to your profile. Gateway International will keep you updated on each step of your application so you can get a final acceptance letter.

Step: Five

Start your Visa Process

Our visa team will assist you throughout the entire visa process from documentation to the visa interview, we take care of it all for you!
